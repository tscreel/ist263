Ingredients

1 (4-lb.) round roast 1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary 1 tbsp. chopped fresh thyme leaves 4 tsp. kosher salt 1 tsp. freshly ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1 Recommended: let roast sit uncovered on a wire rack set in a baking sheet in the refrigerator 1 hour, up to overnight. Step 2 Preheat oven to 450°. In a small bowl, combine oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Rub all over roast. Step 3 Place roast in roasting pan fitted with a roasting rack. Roast for 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 325° and roast 1 hours 45 minutes more for medium, or 2 hours for medium well done. Step 4 Remove from oven and let rest 15 to 30 minutes before serving.
